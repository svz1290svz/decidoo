Decidoo removes the stress from choosing what to eat.

Choose your goal, budget, and meal time. Decidoo evaluates the available options and gives you one clear recommendation instead of an endless list.

FEATURES

• One-tap meal decisions
• Personalized recommendations by meal time, budget, and mood
• Explainable match reasons
• Favorites and recent decision history
• Global food discovery
• English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, and Arabic
• Right-to-left interface support for Arabic
• No account required in version 1.0.0
• No advertising or location tracking in version 1.0.0

DECIDE FASTER

Decidoo is designed around a simple principle: fewer choices, better decisions. The recommendation engine scores food options using your current preferences and reduces repetitive suggestions.

PRIVACY-FIRST RELEASE

Version 1.0.0 processes preferences on the device and does not require account creation. The current release does not transmit personal information to a Decidoo server.

Decidoo is the fastest way to answer one everyday question: What should I eat?
