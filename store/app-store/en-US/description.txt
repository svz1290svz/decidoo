Decidoo makes one everyday decision easier: what should you eat?

Select your meal time, budget, and current goal. Decidoo scores the available food choices and gives you one clear recommendation with simple reasons behind the match.

WHY DECIDOO

• One-tap meal decisions
• Personalized recommendations
• Clear match explanations
• Favorites and recent decision history
• Global food discovery
• Six interface languages
• Arabic right-to-left support
• No account required in version 1.0.0
• No advertising or location tracking in version 1.0.0

Decidoo is designed to reduce decision fatigue. Instead of showing hundreds of options, it helps you move forward with one trusted choice.

Version 1.0.0 processes preferences on the device and does not transmit personal information to a Decidoo server.
