Decidoo version 1.0.0 does not require an account or login.

Core review flow:
1. Open the app.
2. Select a goal, budget, and meal time.
3. Tap the decision button.
4. Review the recommendation and match reasons.
5. Open Explore, History, and Revenue Hub from the bottom navigation.

The Premium, restaurant plan, and sponsored-placement sections are informational product concepts in version 1.0.0. They do not initiate payment, unlock digital content, or transmit purchase data. No external purchase link is presented.

The app does not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, health, photo-library, or tracking permission.
